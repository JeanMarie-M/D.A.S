Features
Everything You Need
Auto Duty Allocation

Automatically assign duties to all eligible students based on class, dorm, form and specialization with fair rotation logic.

Duty Swap System

Students can request duty swaps. Admins review and approve with a full audit trail of every change made.

Instant Reports

Generate duty rosters by class, dorm, area or full school. Print-ready with signature lines for prefects and teachers.

Spreadsheet Import

Bulk import students from Excel or CSV files. The system validates every row and reports errors before saving.

Multi-School Support

Each school registers independently and manages their own data. Schools never see each other's information.

Role-Based Access

Admins have full control. Prefects have limited access. Sensitive actions require admin approval and are fully logged.
